Once Alchemist is installed, this page shows you the basic steps to start shrinking your video library and saving space.

Ready to reclaim some storage space? Let's get Alchemist working on your library.

## Step 1: Library Scan
After setup, Alchemist automatically looks through your folders. You can see what it finds on the **Dashboard**. If you just added a new movie and want Alchemist to see it right away:
- Go to **Settings** > **Library**.
- Click **Trigger Scan**.

## Step 2: Choose a Profile
You can have different settings for different folders. For example, you might want "High Quality" for your favorite movies but "Space Saver" for old TV shows.
- Go to **Settings** > **Profiles**.
- Pick a profile for each of your folders.

## Step 3: Automatic Watching
You can tell Alchemist to keep an eye on your folders. Whenever you add a new file, Alchemist will automatically put it in line to be shrunk.
- Go to **Settings** > **Library**.
- Turn on **Watch folders**.

## Step 4: Intelligent Analysis
Alchemist is smart—it won't waste time on files that are already small. It calculates the **Bits Per Pixel (BPP)** (a measure of how much data is in the image). If the file is already highly compressed, Alchemist will skip it so it doesn't "murder" the quality.

## Step 5: Watch it Work
The **Job Manager** shows you exactly what Alchemist is doing right now. You can see:
- How many frames per second (fps) it's processing.
- How much time is left.
- If it's using your graphics card or your processor.

## Step 6: See Your Savings
The best part! Visit the **Savings Overview** to see a big chart showing exactly how many gigabytes (or terabytes!) of space you've reclaimed.
